Case Study

Cyber Services Website

A service website concept for a cyber shop offering digital assistance, printing, software support, and customer guidance.

Overview

This project translates everyday cyber services into an organized website with clear service categories and contact options.

Features

Service cards, pricing-ready sections, trust copy, contact details, support prompts, and a responsive mobile-first layout.

Technologies

HTMLCSSJavaScriptLocal SEO

Challenges

The challenge was making many small services feel organized and easy for customers to understand.

Lessons Learned

Service websites need clarity, trust, and fast contact options more than decorative complexity.

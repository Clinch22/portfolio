Case Study

Business Landing Page

A conversion-focused landing page concept for a local service business that needs credibility and clear enquiries.

Overview

The landing page gives a business a strong first impression, clear value proposition, and direct contact path.

Features

Hero offer, service blocks, trust section, testimonials, contact call to action, SEO structure, and responsive layout.

Technologies

HTMLCSSSEOAccessibility

Challenges

The challenge was keeping the page persuasive without making it feel overloaded or generic.

Lessons Learned

Small business pages work best when the offer, proof, and contact path are immediately clear.
